What We Are Known For:

Here at Dionysus, we treat everyone as if they are family. We welcome you to our restaurant as if you are arriving at our home.

Chef Kosta arrives as the sun begins to rise every morning to prepare all our dishes from scratch. His helpers, Chris and George, arrive soon after to assist him with the Daily Specials.
