A Little History of How We Got Started

In a little village in Southern Greece, just outside the town of Sparta, Yiayia (grandma) created the most delicious recipes from scratch. These recipes were handed down to her from previous generations, and eventually they made the boat trip to the USA, where Chef Kotcho continues using them daily.

I was taught everything I know from Yiayia in Greece. I would travel there every summer as a young boy, and now I have brought everything I have learned with me back to America.
