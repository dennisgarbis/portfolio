ABOUT ME

How did i end up here?

Top

I have been in the restaurant industry for over 25 years, having done it all: valet to server and bartender to manager. I also opened up a cafe for a couple of years. Returning to my previous employer got me a promotion to general manager.

Covid came, though, and disrupted everything. I saw that the restaurant business was fragile. The hours spent working evenings, holidays, and weekends aren't the best. Covid had us shut down for seven weeks, which gave me the free time to see if there was anything else I could do.

So I thought about going back to school.

Computers have always been an interest of mine. I did some research, and Web Development caught my eye. I had taken courses in college years ago, and those credits were transferrable, allowing me to graduate faster. So I decided to re-enroll in school for the fall semester in 2020. I would take a few courses each semester and work full time while completing my Associate's Degree.

Two years later, I did the unimaginable; graduate. If you would mention it to my friends and family, they would never believe you. Dennis going back to school and graduating with a degree?

Yet here I am.

The first step of my career change is complete. The next step would be to take what I have learned through school and my work experience and finish what I set out to do.

Complete my career change.
