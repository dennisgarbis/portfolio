Mode Fitness

This project had us creating a landing page for a business. I've seen plenty of examples of these pages but never gave them a second thought about their purpose. The purpose of a landing page is to keep the user on your page, with as few external links available, trying to get them to click on a call-to-action button and convert them into a customer. I hope my site accomplishes that.
